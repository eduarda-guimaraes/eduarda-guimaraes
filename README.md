## 💻 About Me
I am a **QA Analyst based in Brazil**, with a background in software development.  
I started my journey as an intern and was **hired as a full-time QA**, gaining hands-on experience in real-world testing scenarios and software quality processes.

I work with **manual testing**, test scenario creation, validation of business rules, and bug tracking, always focusing on delivering reliable and high-quality software.

Currently, I am expanding my skills in **test automation with Cypress** and deepening my knowledge in **web applications and software quality practices**.
